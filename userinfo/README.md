# userinfo/ — put your private source documents here

Drop your existing resume, self-introduction essays, career notes, awards,
certificates, project docs, and job-post captures under this directory.

Suggested layout:

- `raw/`: original personal documents (`pdf`, `docx`, `xlsx`, `pptx`, `md`,
  `txt`, `hwp`, exported Notion pages, screenshots, etc.).
- `job_posts/`: copied job descriptions, saved PDFs, screenshots, or crawl
  fallbacks for company postings.

Everything under `userinfo/raw/` and `userinfo/job_posts/` is ignored by git.
Keep only `.gitkeep` placeholders in the public repository.
